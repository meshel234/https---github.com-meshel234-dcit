Become a Frontend Web Developer


Being a front-end developer is akin to being an architect for websites and apps. Your responsibility is to construct and uphold what users observe and engage with on a website, encompassing everything from the overall layout to the responsive behavior of buttons upon user interaction. As a front-end developer, your role involves bringing ideas and designs to life.

The program commences with Dcit Prelim, a 3-week module designed to equip you for success in the digital workforce. During this phase, you will acquire essential communication, leadership, and career-building skills, setting the foundation for your evolution into a well-rounded professional.

Subsequently, you'll delve into the fundamentals of coding using HTML, CSS, and Javascript, with a simultaneous focus on crafting user-friendly website designs. Practical projects interspersed throughout the program lead up to a capstone project where you'll showcase your skills by publishing your own website.

Regardless of your current skill level, this program is structured to guide you from a novice to a job-ready developer within a span of 3 months.
